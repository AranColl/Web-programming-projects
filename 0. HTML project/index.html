PORTUGAL

Oporto. One of the most beautiful cities in Portugal, and I dare to say in Europe. This was the first stop of my incredible trip to Europe and it was totally worth it. The history of a Port that ends up as a city and a portuguese symbol. Oporto is a must if you ever think of travelling to Europe and their cuisine won't disappoint our most exquisite chefs. Eu amo Portugal!

SPAIN

Barcelona. If you imagine have ever imagined a perfect city, well Barcelona goes beyond. Nice weather throughout all year, awesome people and culture, and I have just started. The city itself is amazing. Some neighbourhoods are even grid structured and activities are being held all the time for everyone. Certainly, the city I will be living for the rest of my life. Estimo Barcelona!

FRANCE

Marseille, Monaco, Lyon. And if that was not enough, Paris. Visiting the most famous french cities and getting to know their traditions and history. A powerful european country and the inventor of croissants and baguettes, coincidence? Click and let me show you my journey. J'aime la France.
